About this page

This page is dedicated to showcasing a project aimed at raising awareness about Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD). It allows users to input simple information and receive insights based on a trained classification model. The goal is to provide a user-friendly tool that promotes understanding and encourages early screening for autism.

  • An easy-to-use interface for inputting information
  • Quick and reliable insights based on your inputs.
  • A responsive design that works seamlessly on all devices.
  • Smooth navigation with clickable links that guide you through the page.

Services We Offer

Our platform is designed to help individuals understand their potential Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) status through a quick and user-friendly interface.

  • Self-Assessment Tool: Users can input their personal and behavioral information through an easy-to-navigate form. This includes scores for specific behaviors, demographic data, and relevant health history.
  • AI-Powered Insights: Using a trained machine learning model, we provide predictions based on your inputs. This allows for an initial understanding of the likelihood of autism.
  • Educational Purpose: Our goal is to promote awareness and encourage early screening. This tool is not a substitute for professional diagnosis but serves as an informative starting point.
  • Privacy and Security: We prioritize user privacy. All inputs are securely processed and are not stored after the session ends.
  • Interactive Design: The platform features a responsive, visually appealing interface that ensures a seamless user experience across devices.

This tool empowers individuals to take proactive steps toward better understanding and addressing autism while raising awareness within communities.
